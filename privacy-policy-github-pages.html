Privacy Policy

LogLogic · Last updated: April 29, 2026

This policy describes how Xu Xiuzhen (“we,” “us,” or “Developer”) handles information when you use the LogLogic mobile application (the “App”) on Android. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. What LogLogic does

LogLogic is a professional productivity companion. It helps you capture and organize short work “clues” (notes and optional voice clips), generate AI-assisted daily and periodic reports based on your content, sync data across devices when signed in, and optionally offer subscriptions via Google Play. Features available to you depend on your account type and subscription.

2. Data we collect

  • Account & authentication. If you sign in, we receive identifiers from your chosen provider (such as Google or Apple Sign-In) and basic profile data as permitted by that provider.
  • Content you create. Text notes, clues, transcripts, attachments you upload or link, report drafts, and related metadata needed to operate the App.
  • Voice (optional). When you choose to record, audio may be processed to provide transcripts and features you request.
  • Subscription & billing status. Purchase or entitlement state provided by Google Play (and our payment partner, where applicable) to unlock paid features — not your full card number.
  • Technical & diagnostics. Device/app version, crash or performance data, and similar information to keep the App reliable and secure.

3. How we use data

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App (including AI features and cloud sync).
  • Authenticate you, protect accounts, and prevent abuse or fraud.
  • Process subscriptions and show correct membership features.
  • Respond to support requests and legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your private content to train public or third-party foundation models.

4. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Where applicable, we rely on: performance of a contract (providing the App), legitimate interests (security, improvement, abuse prevention), consent (where required, e.g. certain optional processing), and legal obligation.

5. Storage, processing & transfers

Data is processed on secure cloud infrastructure (for example, managed backend and storage). We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) for network communication. If data is stored or processed in countries other than your own, we take steps consistent with applicable law and this policy.

6. Third parties

The App relies on service providers that process data on our instructions, for example:

  • Cloud hosting, database, and storage for accounts and synced content.
  • Sign-in providers you choose (e.g. Google, Apple).
  • AI inference providers for report generation, under strict operational controls.
  • Google Play and, where used, in-app purchase/payment infrastructure to validate subscriptions.

Those providers are required to protect information and use it only to provide their services to us.

7. Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the App, comply with law, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When you delete your account (where available in the App), we delete or anonymize associated personal data within a reasonable period, subject to legal exceptions.

8. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures appropriate to the nature of the data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, or withdraw consent where processing is consent-based.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format where applicable.
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

You can exercise many choices inside the App (including account deletion where offered). For privacy-related questions or requests, please use the in-app feedback channel described below.

10. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please reach out through the App’s feedback feature and we will promptly review and delete such information where appropriate.

11. Android permissions

The App requests only what is needed for core features. As declared for this build:

  • Internet — connect to sign-in, sync, AI, and update services.
  • Microphone (record audio) — only when you record voice clues; you can deny and still use non-voice features where available.

Other capabilities (e.g. opening files or email) may use system components without extra sensitive permissions, or may be added in future updates; we will update this policy when collection practices change materially.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new version at this page and change the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy, unless applicable law requires additional steps.
